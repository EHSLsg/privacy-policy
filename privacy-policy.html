Privacy Policy for SG-MY Traffic

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

SG-MY Traffic ("App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy applies to users in Singapore, Malaysia, and other jurisdictions. We comply with:

  • Google Play Store Privacy Requirements
  • Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2012
  • Malaysia Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Location Data

  • What we collect: Your GPS location coordinates when you use the traffic tracking features
  • How we collect it: Through your device's GPS sensor (with your permission)
  • Why we collect it: To calculate travel times from your current location to Malaysia Immigration checkpoints (Tuas and Woodlands routes)
  • Your control: You can enable/disable location access in your device settings at any time

Crowdsourced Traffic Data (Optional)

  • What we collect: Your vehicle speed and timestamp (if you opt-in to crowdsourcing)
  • How we collect it: By analyzing your GPS movement speed when you're on the tracked routes
  • Why we collect it: To build real-time traffic heatmaps and improve travel time predictions for all users
  • Your control: You can enable/disable crowdsourcing in the App settings. Crowdsourcing is opt-in only and completely voluntary

Bluetooth Data (Optional)

  • What we collect: Nearby Bluetooth device counts (if you opt-in to Bluetooth detection)
  • How we collect it: By scanning for nearby Bluetooth devices when you're near the tracked routes
  • Why we collect it: To detect traffic congestion in areas with poor GPS signal (tunnels, underground roads)
  • Your control: You can enable/disable Bluetooth detection in the App settings. Bluetooth scanning is opt-in only

Camera Feeds

  • What we collect: We do not collect or store camera feed data
  • How we use it: We display live camera feeds from the Land Transport Authority (LTA) Singapore for informational purposes only
  • Your control: Camera feeds are provided by LTA and are not stored by us

Device Information

  • What we collect: Device model, OS version, app version, crash logs
  • How we collect it: Automatically through app analytics
  • Why we collect it: To diagnose crashes, improve app performance, and ensure compatibility
  • Your control: You can disable analytics in your device settings

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Analytics

  • What we collect: Which screens you view, features you use, time spent in app
  • How we collect it: Through app analytics (Expo Analytics)
  • Why we collect it: To understand user behavior and improve the app
  • Your control: You can opt-out in app settings

3. Data Anonymization & Aggregation

3.1 How We Protect Your Privacy

Crowdsourced Data Processing:

  • Your speed data is anonymized immediately upon collection
  • We do not collect your name, phone number, email, or any identifying information
  • Your data is assigned a random anonymous ID that cannot be traced back to you
  • Individual data points are deleted after 24-48 hours
  • Only aggregated statistics are retained (e.g., "average speed on Route X at Hour Y")

3.2 Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Crowdsourced Speed Data 24-48 hours Real-time traffic only
Bluetooth Counts 24-48 hours Real-time congestion only
GPS Coordinates Real-time only Not stored permanently
Aggregated Statistics 90 days Traffic pattern analysis
Crash Logs 30 days Bug fixing
Analytics 12 months Usage trends
Device ID Until app uninstall App functionality

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Core App Functionality

  • Calculate travel times from your location to immigration checkpoints
  • Display real-time traffic conditions on the Tuas and Woodlands routes
  • Show live camera feeds from LTA
  • Display 7-day congestion forecasts and 24-hour timing trends

4.2 What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for targeted advertising
  • ❌ We do NOT track your identity across apps
  • ❌ We do NOT share crowdsourced data with government agencies

5. Your Rights & Choices

5.1 Singapore PDPA Rights

Under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Right of Access: Request access to your personal data held by us
  • Right of Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications and crowdsourcing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for location tracking

5.2 Malaysia PDPA Rights

Under the Malaysia Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the right to:

  • Right of Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right of Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of processing for direct marketing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for any data processing

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

In Transit:

  • All data transmitted to our servers uses HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.2+)
  • Encrypted communication prevents interception

At Rest:

  • Sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Database access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
